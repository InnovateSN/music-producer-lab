ğŸ­ The Psychology of Tension

Music is a journey of expectations. We create tension by building anticipation, and release by resolving it. This cycle keeps listeners engaged.

  • Harmonic tension: Dominant chords (V) resolve to tonic (I)
  • Rhythmic tension: Syncopation, removed drums, building patterns
  • Dynamic tension: Quiet passages before loud releases
  • Melodic tension: Notes that want to resolve to home notes
  • Silence: The ultimate tension builder before big moments
