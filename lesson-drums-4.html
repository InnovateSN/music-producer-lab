Create a 16th-Note Hi-Hat Groove

Make your beat more energetic by adding 16th-note movement to the hi-hats. You'll add one extra hi-hat hit to create that modern, danceable feel.

What to do:

  1. Kick row: Steps 1, 5, 9, 13 (unchanged).
  2. Snare row: Steps 5 and 13 (unchanged).
  3. Hi-Hat row: Start with eighth notes (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 13, 15) then add step 8.
  4. Step 8 is the 'e' of beat 2 - it adds a subtle push before the snare resolves.
Tip: That extra hi-hat on step 8 creates anticipation. It's a small change that makes a big difference!
Target Pattern:
Kick: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
Snare: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
Hi-Hat: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
The highlighted step 8 is the new 16th-note addition!
