Concept

Understanding Odd Time

Odd time signatures have an uneven number of beats per measure, creating unique rhythmic feels.

  • 5/4: Five quarter notes per bar (3+2 or 2+3 groupings)
  • 7/8: Seven eighth notes (2+2+3 or 3+2+2)
  • 9/8: Nine eighths (often 2+2+2+3)
Technique

Famous Examples

Many classic songs use odd time signatures effectively.

  • "Take Five" - 5/4 time signature
  • "Money" (Pink Floyd) - 7/4
  • "Schism" (Tool) - Mixed meters
