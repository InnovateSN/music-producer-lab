Create a Fill at the End of the Bar

Drum fills add excitement and signal transitions in music. In this exercise, you'll create a fill pattern in the last beat (beat 4) while keeping the groove in beats 1-3.

What to do:

  1. Kick row: Steps 1, 5, 9, 13, 15 - normal pattern plus extra kicks in beat 4.
  2. Snare row: Steps 5, 13, 16 - backbeat plus a final hit on step 16.
  3. Hi-Hat row: Steps 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11 only - stop the hi-hats before the fill.
  4. The fill creates tension before the loop restarts!
Tip: Fills 'break' the pattern to signal something new is coming. The silence in the hi-hats makes the kick/snare fill pop!
Target Pattern:
Kick: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
Snare: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
Hi-Hat: 1234 5678 9101112 13141516
Steps 12-16 are the "fill zone" - hi-hats stop to let kick/snare shine!
