The Mixing Chain

Professional drum mixing follows a specific signal flow for best results.

  • EQ first: Shape the tone, remove mud (200-400Hz)
  • Compression: Control dynamics, add punch
  • Saturation: Add warmth and harmonic content
  • Parallel: Blend heavily compressed signal with dry
Technique

EQ Guidelines by Drum

Each drum element has specific frequency considerations.

  • Kick: Sub (50-60Hz), punch (100-120Hz), click (2-4kHz)
  • Snare: Body (200-250Hz), crack (2-5kHz), air (10kHz+)
  • Hi-Hat: Cut mud (below 300Hz), presence (8-12kHz)
