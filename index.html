Core Modules

Six production pillars, from rhythm to final master

Each module covers specific, testable skills: kick placement, sidechain compression, stereo width, loudness targets. Build your foundation, then layer in advanced techniques.

Drums & Rhythm

Program 4-on-the-floor patterns, offbeat hi-hats, and ghost snares. Learn swing timing, velocity layering, and rhythmic fills that drive energy.

Build Your First Beat (8 min)

Arrangement

Structure 8-bar intros, 16-bar buildups, and dynamic drops. Practice filtering, automation curves, and tension/release arcs for electronic and pop formats.

Structure a Full Track (12 min)

Sound Design

Shape sounds with oscillators, filters (low-pass, high-pass), ADSR envelopes, and LFOs. Layer samples, add distortion, and design bass, leads, and pads from scratch.

Design Your First Synth (10 min)

Mixing

Balance levels, carve space with EQ (cutting mud at 200-400Hz), apply compression ratios (4:1 for drums), and place elements in stereo field with panning and reverb sends.

Mix a Clean Balance (15 min)

Vocals

De-ess harsh frequencies, compress dynamics (2-4dB gain reduction), add doubles and harmonies, apply delay/reverb for depth. Make vocals clear and present without overpowering the mix.

Process a Vocal Track (12 min)

Mastering

Hit -14 LUFS for Spotify, apply multiband compression, stereo widening (keep bass mono), and peak limiting (0.3dB headroom). Prepare tracks for streaming and distribution.

Master for Streaming (10 min)
