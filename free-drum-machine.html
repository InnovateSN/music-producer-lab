Free Online Drum Machine: Everything You Need to Know

What is a 16-step drum sequencer?

A 16-step sequencer divides one bar of music into 16 equal parts, giving you precise control over when each drum hit occurs. This is the same layout used in classic drum machines like the Roland TR-808 and TR-909. It's the standard for electronic music production.

Can I export my drum loops?

Yes! Click the "Export WAV" button to download your loop as a high-quality audio file. Import it into any DAW (Ableton, FL Studio, Logic Pro, etc.) or use it in your productions. The export includes all your mixer settings, velocity changes, and humanization.

Do I need to create an account?

No. This is a completely free online drum machine with no signup required. Just open it and start making beats. We believe in removing barriers between you and making music.

What's the difference between this and other beat makers?

Most online drum machines are toys. This one is a learning tool with professional features: per-step velocity control, swing, humanization, a real mixer, and WAV export. It's part of our drum programming curriculum, designed to help you build real skills.
