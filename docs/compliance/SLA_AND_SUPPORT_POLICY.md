# SLA & Support Policy (Institutional)

**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Service Availability Target
- Monthly uptime target: **99.9%** for production service.
- Excludes: scheduled maintenance windows (announced in advance), force majeure, and customer-side misconfiguration.

## 2. Support Channels
- Primary: support email / ticketing queue.
- Secondary: escalation contact for P1/P2 incidents.

## 3. Incident Severity Matrix
- **P1 (Critical outage / data exposure):** initial response within 1 hour.
- **P2 (Major functional degradation):** initial response within 4 hours.
- **P3 (Non-critical defect):** initial response within 1 business day.
- **P4 (General request):** initial response within 2 business days.

## 4. Service Credits
- <99.9% and >=99.0%: 5% monthly fee credit.
- <99.0% and >=98.0%: 10% monthly fee credit.
- <98.0%: 20% monthly fee credit.

## 5. Planned Maintenance
- Standard maintenance window communicated at least 72 hours in advance.
- Emergency maintenance communicated as soon as practicable.

## 6. Monitoring & Reporting
- Platform health checks and incident logs retained.
- Monthly availability report available on request for institutional customers.
