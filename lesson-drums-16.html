Concept

What is Drum Layering?

Layering combines multiple samples to create one unified sound with more depth and character.

  • Sub layer: Low frequencies for weight and punch
  • Body layer: Mid frequencies for tone and character
  • Top layer: High frequencies for attack and presence
Technique

Layering Best Practices

Effective layering requires careful attention to phase alignment and frequency balance.

  • Check phase alignment between layers
  • Use EQ to carve space for each layer
  • Compress the layered result for glue
