FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work for schools?

We offer a 3-month free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Email us to request access, and we'll set up your school account within 24 hours.

Do I need to install any software?

No! Music Producer Lab is 100% browser-based. Students access everything through Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Works on Windows, Mac, Chromebooks, and tablets.

What's included in the lessons?

Our curriculum covers Drums & Rhythm, Harmony & Melody, Music Theory, Ear Training, Bass, Arrangement, Sound Design, Mixing, Vocals, and Mastering. Each lesson includes interactive tools and practical exercises.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Individual plans can be cancelled anytime with immediate effect. School plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. No penalties, no questions asked.

Is this suitable for GCSE/A-Level?

Yes! Our curriculum aligns with GCSE and A-Level Music Technology specifications. Students learn production techniques, music theory, mixing, and mastering - all key components of exam boards like Edexcel, AQA, and OCR.

What payment methods do you accept?

For individuals: PayPal, credit/debit cards. For schools: bank transfer (BACS), purchase orders, and invoicing with 30-day payment terms.
