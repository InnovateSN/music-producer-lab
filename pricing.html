Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

We offer a 3-month free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Simply email us to request access, and we'll set up your school account within 24 hours.

What's included in the 175 lessons?

Our curriculum covers 10 core modules: Drums & Rhythm, Harmony & Melody, Music Theory, Ear Training, Bass & Low End, Arrangement, Sound Design, Mixing, Vocals, and Mastering. Each lesson includes interactive tools, practical exercises, and theory.

Do we need to install any software?

No! Music Producer Lab is 100% cloud-based. Students access everything through their web browser. Works on Windows, Mac, Chromebooks, and tablets. No installation, no IT headaches.

Can we add more students later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll prorate the cost for the remainder of your billing period. Contact us for flexible pricing if you need custom student limits.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, no hidden costs. The price you see is what you pay. We'll have you up and running within 24 hours of signing up.

How do teachers track student progress?

Teachers get a comprehensive dashboard showing each student's progress, time spent, completed lessons, and achievement certificates. You can export reports in CSV format for school records.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer (BACS), school purchase orders, and invoicing. We'll send you an invoice after your free trial ends, and you have 30 days to pay.

What if we want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No penalties, no questions asked. We want you to stay because you love the platform, not because you're locked in.

Is this suitable for GCSE/A-Level Music Technology?

Yes! Our curriculum aligns with GCSE and A-Level Music Technology specifications. Students learn production techniques, music theory, mixing, and mastering - all key components of exam boards like Edexcel, AQA, and OCR.

Do you offer discounts for MATs (Multi-Academy Trusts)?

Yes! We offer special pricing for Multi-Academy Trusts and school groups. Contact us at innovatesolutionsnow@gmail.com for a custom quote.
