Concept

Arrangement for Sync & Media

Create music specifically designed for licensing in visual media, with multiple versions and stems that give editors maximum flexibility.

  • Multiple versions: Full, 60s, 30s, 15s, and stinger edits
  • Stem delivery: Separated instrument groups for mixing
  • Licensing requirements: Industry-standard formats and specs
Technique

Creating Sync-Ready Arrangements

Structure your arrangements to be easily editable and adaptable for various media placements and timing requirements.

  • Clear sections: Distinct intro, build, climax, and outro
  • Edit points: Natural places to cut for different lengths
  • Instrumental options: Versions with and without vocals
Application

Key Takeaways

Master sync and media arrangement techniques to monetize your music through licensing opportunities.

  • Understanding sync licensing fundamentals
  • Creating professional stems and versions
  • Meeting industry delivery standards
