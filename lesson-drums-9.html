Understanding Swing

Swing (also called "groove" or "shuffle") delays every other 16th note slightly, creating a "bouncy" feel. In hip-hop, this is essential for that head-nod groove.

Swing Comparison

No Swing (Straight): All notes perfectly on-grid

With Swing (50-60%): Off-beats pushed back

ğŸ’¡ The delayed off-beats create the characteristic "bounce" of hip-hop
