Genre fusion takes elements from two or more styles and combines them thoughtfully. The best fusions don't feel forced‚Äîthey create something new and exciting while honoring the source genres.

  • Identify core elements: What makes each genre unique?
  • Find common ground: Where do the genres naturally overlap?
  • Balance proportions: 50/50 isn't always best‚Äîexperiment with ratios
  • Maintain groove: The fusion should still feel danceable/musical
  • Add your voice: Personal touches make fusions authentic
