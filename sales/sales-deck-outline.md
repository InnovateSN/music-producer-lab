## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary:** #667eea (purple-blue)
- **Secondary:** #764ba2 (deep purple)
- **Accent:** #10b981 (green for checkmarks/success)
- **Text:** #333 (dark gray)
- **Background:** White or light gray (#f9fafb)

### Fonts
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins)
- **Body:** Regular, sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans)
- **Sizes:**
  - Slide title: 36-40pt
  - Body text: 18-20pt
  - Small text: 14-16pt

### Images
- High-quality screenshots (1920x1080 minimum)
- Consistent interface theme (match website)
- Avoid stock photos (use actual product screenshots)
- Add subtle drop shadows to screenshots

## Presentation Tips

**Opening (Slides 1-2):**
- Start with a question: "Who here has tried buying DAW licenses for 200 students?"
- Acknowledge the pain point before showing solution

**Middle (Slides 3-8):**
- Show live demo if possible (more engaging than static slides)
- Click through actual platform during slides 4-6
- Ask if they have questions as you go

**Pricing (Slide 9):**
- Don't apologize for price
- Emphasize ROI and savings (slide 10)
- Highlight free trial (removes risk)

**Closing (Slides 11-12):**
- Recap benefits
- Strong CTA: "Should we start your free trial today?"
- Get calendar out: "When's a good time for a kickoff call?"
