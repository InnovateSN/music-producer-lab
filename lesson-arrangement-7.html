Practice

Exercise: Build a Hip-Hop Arrangement

Create a verse-hook structure with space for vocals and energy variation.

  1. Intro (4 bars): Just the beat with minimal elements.
  2. Verse 1 (16 bars): Full drums, simpler melody, room for vocals.
  3. Hook (8 bars): Add more melodic elements, fuller 808.
  4. Verse 2 (16 bars): Add subtle variations from verse 1.
  5. Bridge (8 bars): Beat switch or stripped-back section.
Summary

Key Takeaways

  • Verse-hook-verse: The fundamental hip-hop structure
  • Leave space: Verses need room for vocals
  • Hooks = energy: Fuller arrangement on the hook
  • Beat switches: Add surprise and keep listeners engaged
